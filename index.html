Baubau - The Port city of Southeast Sulawesi

About

Baubau is a second largest city in Southeast Sulawesi Province, Indonesia. It is located in Buton island, which is on the southwest cost of Buton. This city attained its city status on 2001.

People

Baubau is the cultural center of the Butonese people, the former capital of Sultanese of Buton. The native Butonese people speaks in Wolio language. The population is about 165.000 people.
